About

This application is a dating platform that combines clustering and content-based filtering using Spotify data. Users can connect their Spotify account to share their top tracks and artists, making matches more personalized and enjoyable.

The app supports light and dark modes, offers the ability to listen to matches' favorite tracks, and ensures a seamless and fun user experience.
